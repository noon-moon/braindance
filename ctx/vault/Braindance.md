Braindance is a templated meta-repository implementing an Obsidian vault alongside LLM-harness-agnostic skills and lifecycle tooling. The idea is that a developer maintains one `braindance` repo as their personal knowledge and workflow layer, then clones any repository they're working on into `repo/` to bring that context to bear on agentic development sessions.
